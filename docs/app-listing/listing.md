## Full description
Pixel Kicks Tracking sends your store's events to GA4, Meta (Conversions API) and a server-side GTM container from the server, not the browser — so your data keeps flowing past ad blockers, Safari ITP and the Checkout Extensibility sandbox that breaks theme-based tracking.

Built on Shopify's Web Pixels API, it covers the events theme scripts can no longer reach (including checkout and purchase) and adds the things most trackers miss:

- **Server-side delivery** to GA4 Measurement Protocol, Meta Conversions API, and server-side GTM, with built-in event deduplication (transaction_id for GA4, event_id for Meta).
- **High Meta Event Match Quality** — hashes email, phone, name and address, and forwards fbp/fbc, to lift match rates.
- **Google Consent Mode v2** — sends consent-flagged events so GA4 can model conversions where consent is declined, and skips marketing destinations without consent.
- **Subscription conversion tracking** — fires a server-side subscription event from paid orders, with first-order attribution carried onto recurring orders.
- **Refund & cancellation tracking** — nets refunds off your conversions so campaigns stop optimising toward returns.
- **SEO engagement** — scroll depth, engaged-content views and complete site-search events for GA4/GTM.
- **Bot filtering** — drops crawler/headless traffic before it reaches your ad platforms.
- **Delivery health + an event sandbox** — preview exact payloads, send live test events, and see per-destination delivery success at a glance.

No theme code, no gtag/fbq snippets, no per-order pricing.

## Key benefits (3 bullets)
1. Recover the 20–40% of conversions lost to ad blockers, ITP and the checkout sandbox — delivered server-side.
2. Higher Meta match quality and GA4 accuracy, with proper deduplication and Consent Mode v2.
3. Track what others can't: subscriptions, refunds, and on-site SEO engagement — verifiable before you trust it.
